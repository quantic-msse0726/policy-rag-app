Code of Conduct

Scope

This Code of Conduct outlines the standards of behavior expected of all employees, contractors, and representatives. It ensures a respectful, inclusive, and professional workplace. Related policies include Harassment Prevention, Confidentiality and NDA, and Acceptable Use.

Definitions

  • Integrity: Acting honestly, transparently, and consistently with our values.
  • Retaliation: Adverse action against an individual for reporting concerns or participating in investigations.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Act honestly and transparently in all business dealings.
  • Respect: Treat everyone with dignity, regardless of role, background, or perspective.
  • Accountability: Take responsibility for your actions and their outcomes.
  • Collaboration: Work constructively with colleagues to achieve shared goals.
  • Excellence: Strive for high quality in your work and continuous improvement.

Professional Conduct

  • Communicate professionally in writing, speech, and conduct.
  • Resolve conflicts through constructive dialogue and escalation when needed.
  • Maintain appropriate boundaries between personal and professional life.
  • Do not engage in harassment, discrimination, or retaliation. See Harassment Prevention Policy.
  • Protect confidential information. See Confidentiality and NDA Policy.
  • Ensure records and communications are accurate; do not falsify documents.

Conflicts of Interest

Disclose any personal, financial, or other interest that could conflict with your duties. Do not use your position for personal gain.

Reporting Violations

Report suspected violations to your manager, HR, or the Ethics Hotline. Reports may be anonymous where permitted. Retaliation against good-faith reporters is prohibited. Investigations are conducted promptly and fairly.

Enforcement

Violations may result in corrective action up to and including termination. Legal action may be pursued where appropriate.
